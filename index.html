Formula: 0.50 × peak_sev × 20 + 0.50 × avg_sev × 20
🌡️
Dual-Weight Composite
Peak severity captures tail-risk events that drive catastrophic losses. Average severity captures chronic chronic stress on operations. Equal 50/50 weighting ensures both dimensions matter.
🔬
CMIP6 Ensemble Averaging
31–35 climate models per SSP scenario are bias-corrected and averaged. Bootstrap resampling produces 95% confidence intervals — so you see the range, not just the point estimate.
🏭
Sector-Specific Vulnerability
A copper mine responds to heat differently than a data centre. Each sector has calibrated exposure multipliers derived from peer-reviewed damage function literature.
📐
Auditable Methodology
Every coefficient, source dataset, and formula step is cited and reviewable. TCFD-aligned disclosure-ready output with full provenance chain from raw data to score.
